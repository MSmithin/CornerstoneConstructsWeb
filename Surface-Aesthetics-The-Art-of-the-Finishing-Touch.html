Surface & Aesthetics

The Art of the Finishing Touch

You’ve built the walls and laid the floors — now it’s time to make your home truly yours. The "Surface & Aesthetics" stage is where a house becomes a masterpiece. It’s the difference between a room that looks "finished" and a room that looks "designed."

At Cornerstone Constructs, we specialize in the fine details that catch the eye and increase your home’s value. Here are the six ways we can transform your space.

Why Aesthetics is a Smart Investment

  • Instant Modernization: A new backsplash and fresh paint can make a kitchen feel brand new.
  • Reflected Personality: Express your unique style with custom finishes.
  • High ROI: Surface upgrades often deliver the best return when selling your home.

Our Aesthetics Services: The Breakdown

1. Paint Finishes — The "Biggest Impact" Choice

Professional painting with perfect prep work for buttery-smooth results — from ultra-matte to high-gloss.

  • Best For: Any room needing an instant mood shift

2. Wall Treatments & Moldings — The "Architectural Character" Choice

Wainscoting, crown molding, chair rails, and accent walls that add depth and elegance.

  • Best For: Dining rooms, offices, and master bedrooms

3. Wall Textures — The "Depth & Dimension" Choice

Subtle orange peel to luxurious Venetian plaster and skip-trowel finishes.

  • Best For: Great rooms and entryways

4. Countertop Surfaces — The "Centerpiece" Choice

Quartz, granite, and premium laminates with precision installation.

  • Best For: Kitchens and bathrooms

5. Tile & Backsplash — The "Jewelry of the Kitchen" Choice

Subway tile, mosaics, and custom designs that protect and beautify.

  • Best For: Kitchens and bathrooms

6. Architectural Metal Finishes — The "Modern Luxury" Choice

Brushed brass, stainless steel, blackened iron, and custom metal accents.

  • Best For: Contemporary homes and high-end renovations

💡 Pro-Tips for a Designer Look

  • The Fifth Wall Secret: Don’t forget the ceiling! A soft complementary color can make a room feel cozy and professionally designed.
  • Lighting is Everything: Test textures and tiles under your actual room lighting before final selection.
  • Consistency is Key: Coordinate metal finishes with cabinet hardware and light fixtures for a cohesive, intentional look.

Ready to Fall in Love with Your Home Again?

You don’t need a full renovation to create dramatic change. The right surfaces and finishes can transform your space into the home you’ve always dreamed of.

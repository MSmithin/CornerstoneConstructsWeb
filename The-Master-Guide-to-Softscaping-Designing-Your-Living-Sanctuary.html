The Master Guide to Softscaping

Designing Your Living Sanctuary

While hardscaping provides the structure, Softscaping is the heartbeat of your property. It is the texture, the color, and the life that turns a house into a home. A well-designed softscape doesn't just look pretty — it breathes, cools your home, provides privacy, and significantly increases your property’s resale value.

Below is our comprehensive breakdown of every softscaping service we offer. Whether you are starting with a dirt lot or refreshing an aging garden, this guide will help you create your perfect outdoor sanctuary.

1. Lawn Installation and Care

Sodding

Instant mature turf for an immediate lush lawn.

  • Instant gratification and excellent erosion control
  • Perfect for families, pets, and upcoming events

Seeding

Custom seed blends tailored to your sun, shade, and traffic conditions.

  • Most cost-effective for large areas
  • Develops deeper, more resilient roots over time

Grading

Professional leveling and sloping of your land.

  • Critical for proper water drainage away from your foundation
  • Prevents swampy areas and mosquito breeding

2. Planting Services

Trees & Shrubs

High-quality specimens planted at the correct depth with premium soil.

  • Increase property value and provide shade/energy savings
  • Add year-round structure and beauty

Perennials, Annuals & Flower Beds

Thoughtfully designed beds with color that cycles throughout the seasons.

  • Perennials return every year; annuals provide bold seasonal color
  • Create stunning focal points that welcome guests

Hedges & Screening

Dense, fast-growing plants that create natural privacy walls.

  • Block views, reduce noise, and act as windbreaks
  • Far more elegant than traditional fencing

3. Garden Features & Specialized Design

Mulching

Premium organic mulch (cedar, hemlock, etc.) applied in thick, even layers.

  • Suppresses weeds, retains moisture, and regulates soil temperature

Xeriscaping & Ground Covers

Drought-tolerant plants, stones, and low-growing covers for low-maintenance beauty.

  • Greatly reduces water usage and maintenance

Vertical Gardening & Soil Amendment

Trellises, living walls, and custom soil improvements based on pH and nutrient testing.

4. Soil & Site Preparation

Land Clearing & Excavating

Professional removal of brush, stumps, and precise digging for new features.

  • Reclaims usable space and ensures every project starts on solid ground

💡 Pro-Tips for a Stunning Softscape

  • The 5% Slope Rule: Grade your yard to drop 6 inches every 10 feet away from the house to protect your foundation.
  • Mulch Volcanoes: Never pile mulch against tree trunks — leave breathing room to prevent rot.
  • Layering: Combine trees, shrubs, perennials, and ground cover for depth and year-round interest.

Your outdoor space should be a true living sanctuary — an extension of your home that brings joy every single day.
